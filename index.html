Data Science Projects


Geospatial Analysis of Gentrification on NYC

A geo-spatial analysis of social and economical variables that have been linked to the process of gentrification in the literature. New York is used as taste case. Done as final project for GIS Spatial Analysis using QGIS, R, d3 and CartoDb. Written report located here.

Unusual Pets in NYC

Winner of MoMA's Art Datathon event. We utilized the museum's collection data to explore the most exhibited artists and genres through time, revealing interesting patterns on their selection.
