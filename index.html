Data Science Projects


Geospatial Analysis of Gentrification on NYC

A geo-spatial analysis of social and economical variables that have been linked to the process of gentrification on NYC. Done as final project for GIS Spatial Analysis using QGIS, R, d3 and CartoDb. Written report located here.

MoMA Through Time

Winner of MoMA's Art Datathon event. We utilized the museum's collection data to explore the most exhibited artists and genres through time, revealing interesting patterns on their selection.


Unusual Pets in NYC

Part of a larger project analyzing NYC's 311 reports data. This map shows exotic animal related complaints and the place where they occured.

CULPA Sentiment

Sentiment analysis and visualization of Columbia's academic course ratings. Won prize for best use of the API.

Data Apps



GeoTracking App

Built for Trinnacle Capital Management. Uses mobile geolocation data to fit a linear model based on user parameters and predict companies' revenue.


Process Monitor

Built for Kora Management LP . Monitors the status of several web-scraping processes and provides drill-down details on them.


Twitter Brand Monitor

Collects tweets related to a company and its products, performs sentiment analysis on it, and extracts user-ratings for them. The data is presented as time-series with drill-down options.
